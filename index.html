Origins

Long Island Latino Dominant Origins by Census Tract, 2017

A common misconception is that Latinos are a homogenous group of individuals sharing the same origin and culture, which is not the case. Rather, Latinos are a radically heterogeneous group with respect to language, race, beliefs, traditions, culture, and national origins. This mapping applications allows for the visualization and comparison of the differing groups which make up "Latinos" by census tract and other aggregations. Tracts are colored according to the "dominant" origin within that tract. The more dominant an origin is with respect to total latino population of a tract group, the darker the color appears. Click on different census tracts to compare!
